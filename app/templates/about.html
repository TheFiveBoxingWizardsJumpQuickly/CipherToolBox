Crypto Brella

Cipher Toolbox

CryptoBrella is a long-term personal project created by a3xgvo, with the following goals;

- To provide readable and user-friendly web-based tools for various ciphers

- To provide documentation, references, and efficient test cases

- To provide attractive cipher challenges


The name CryptoBrella is inspired by the SplatBrella from the game Splatoon. It is a parasol-looking shotgun that I used to bring the most.

The SplatBrella is an umbrella that splats inklings.

CryptoBrella is not an umbrella, but it decrypts hidden things.

OK, it does not really matter.


Regarding the cipher challenges, the original challenges and the Ingress Passcode Challenge are available. These challenges range from simple puzzles to more advanced and complex ciphers, catering to novice and experienced users.

Regarding the cipher tools, over 50 ciphers and tools are available as of 2023/3/5. I am continuously working on adding more tools.

The documentation part is outstanding...


CryptoBrella is a constantly evolving project, with new features and updates being added regularly.

The changes will be applied without prior notice.
